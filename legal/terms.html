01Service Description

AgentGuard provides a cloud-hosted API platform for AI agent security — including runtime policy enforcement, tool call interception and auditing, deployment validation, and compliance reporting ("the Service"). The Service is designed to wrap AI agent infrastructure and evaluate tool calls against customer-defined security policies before they are executed.

The Service includes:

  • Policy Engine: Evaluates inbound tool call requests against YAML/JSON policy rules and returns allow/deny/modify decisions in real time.
  • Audit Trail: Maintains a tamper-evident, hash-chained log of all policy evaluations and decisions.
  • Developer Dashboard: A web interface for managing policies, reviewing audit logs, and generating compliance reports.
  • SDKs: Client libraries for integrating the Service into AI agent frameworks (Python SDK; additional languages planned).
  • Compliance Templates: Pre-built policy bundles aligned to regulatory frameworks including the EU AI Act, OWASP Agentic Top 10, and others.

We may add, modify, or discontinue features of the Service at any time. Where practical, we will provide reasonable advance notice of material changes.
