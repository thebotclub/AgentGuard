## FAQ

**Q: Can I upgrade or downgrade at any time?**  
Yes. Plan changes take effect immediately. Downgrade refunds are prorated on annual plans.

**Q: What counts as an "agent"?**  
An agent is any autonomous AI process registered with AgentGuard — a LangChain agent, a CrewAI crew member, an OpenAI Assistants thread runner, or your own custom agent implementation. Batch jobs and single-turn completions don't count.

**Q: What happens if I exceed my event limit?**  
You'll receive a notification at 80% and 100% of your monthly limit. Events above the cap are queued and evaluated as capacity allows — no data is lost. Upgrade at any time to restore full throughput.

**Q: Is there a free trial for paid plans?**  
Yes. Team and Business both offer 14-day free trials with full feature access — no credit card required. Enterprise trials are available with a signed POC agreement.

**Q: Does the self-hosted option include all features?**  
Yes. Self-hosted Enterprise includes the full AgentGuard feature set deployed to your infrastructure. Updates, support, and compliance materials are covered by your Enterprise contract.

**Q: Can I use AgentGuard in an air-gapped environment?**  
Yes — Enterprise self-hosted supports air-gapped deployment with an offline plugin bundle and local-only audit log storage. Contact sales for the air-gapped installation guide.

**Q: Do you have a HIPAA BAA?**  
Yes. AgentGuard offers HIPAA Business Associate Agreements (BAAs) for Enterprise customers in healthcare. Contact sales to initiate a BAA as part of your Enterprise agreement.

**Q: Is there academic or open-source pricing?**  
Yes. We offer free Business tier access for qualifying open-source projects and academic institutions. Apply via our GitHub page.
