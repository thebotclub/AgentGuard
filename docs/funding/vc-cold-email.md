## Usage Notes

- **Personalise the first line.** Reference a portfolio company, a post they wrote, or a thesis they've published. Generic cold emails get skimmed.
- **Send Tuesday–Thursday, 9–11am recipient time.** Highest open rates for cold outreach.
- **Follow up once, 5 days later.** One line: *"Following up on AgentGuard — happy to send the memo if helpful."* Do not send a third email.
- **Attach nothing.** The email is the hook. The demo link is the product. Send the memo only if they respond.
- **Target partners, not associates.** Associates forward things. Partners make decisions.
